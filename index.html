State Forge

Pick any of Melee’s 341 universal action states - one or several per overlay - choose a color, and forge a module for them. Forged modules attach to your Master Hook through a one-word extension patch and stack with everything above; stock overlays keep priority when states overlap.

3 - Your Master Hook

Modules pack from 0x803FB800 - a single-state module is 0x64 bytes and each extra state adds one compare pair, so slot addresses shift as the list changes. The patch line redirects the hub’s final blr into an extension hub at 0x80002280 that calls your modules, so paste the forge pack after the Master Hook in your .ini.
